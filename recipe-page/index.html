Vietnamese Spring Rolls

Only way my mom got me to eat my veggies. Light and healthy spring rolls, filled with shrimp, rice noodles, and fresh herbs/veggies.

  • 1 package of rice wrappers
  • 2 ounces of rice vermicelli noodles
  • 12 cooked shrimps
  • 1 cup cilantro
  • 7 butter lettuce leaves
  • 1 cup bean sprouts
