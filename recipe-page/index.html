Vietnamese Spring Rolls

Only way my mom got me to eat my veggies! Light and healthy spring rolls, filled with shrimp, rice noodles, and fresh herbs/veggies.

Photo credit: RecipeTin Eats

  • 2 ounces of rice vermicelli noodles
  • 1 package of rice wrappers
  • 12 cooked shrimps
  • 1 cup cilantro
  • 1 cup mint
  • 7 butter lettuce leaves
  • 1 cup bean sprouts

Directions:

  1. Bring a pot of water to a boil. Let vermicelli noodles boil for 5 minutes, and drain into a colander and rinse noodles with cold water.
  2. Fill large bowl with hot water and in a circular motion, dip rice wrapper into hot water to soften. Lay soften wrapper on flat surface.
  3. About 2 inches from the bottom, begin layering your ingredients, no particular order. Can begin with lettuce, then a handful of vermicelli noodles, followed by bean sprouts, cilantro, mint, and last would be the shrimp.
  4. Fold in sides first. Then tightly roll the wrapper upwards.
  5. Repeat
